Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Football Media Network ("we", "us") operates an automated football news and highlights service that publishes content to Telegram, X (Twitter), TikTok, and YouTube. This policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

What We Collect

Our public website (this site) does not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or ad networks, and does not collect personal information from visitors beyond standard, anonymized web server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamp) used only for security and reliability.

Our social accounts (Telegram, X, TikTok, YouTube) are subject to the privacy policy of the respective platform. If you contact us directly (e.g. by email), we retain that correspondence only to respond to your inquiry.

Third-Party Platform APIs

We use official publishing APIs (including TikTok's Content Posting API) solely to upload content — match graphics and video recaps generated by our own pipeline — to accounts we own and operate. We do not use these integrations to access, collect, or store other users' personal data, and we do not request permissions beyond what is required to publish our own content.

Data Sources for Content

The football data referenced in our posts (scores, events, standings, news headlines) is sourced from public sports data providers and news outlets, and reflects publicly available match information rather than personal data about individuals beyond public figures' on-pitch performance.

Children's Privacy

Our service is a general-audience sports news outlet and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
